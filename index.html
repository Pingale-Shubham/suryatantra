Welcome to Suryatantra

Empowering PCB Manufacturers with Top-Quality Supplies

The company was incorporated in March 2019, with the primary aim to utilize its state-of-the-art professional grade Printed Circuit Boards Manufacturing, Drilling & Routing process to provide exceptionally high quality and cost-effective products and services to its customers.

After having a good experience of two decades in PCB manufacturing, services and supply chain; we understand the needs of our potential customers and have designed various solutions for Drilling, Routing, and Film Plotting.

We offer automation solutions to PCB manufacturers and provide customers with customized raw materials, to suit their needs. Having partnerships with the best companies across Asia for purchase, we can provide our customers with high-quality consumables for their high-quality products.

Precision Machining Tools & Components for PCB Manufacturing

At Suryatantra, we utilize a wide range of advanced machining tools to ensure precision and efficiency in our PCB manufacturing process. This collection of high-quality tools includes specialized drilling bits, routing blades, and cutting components designed to handle the intricacies of PCB fabrication.

These tools enable accurate drilling, cutting, and shaping of circuit boards, ensuring top-notch quality and performance for our clients. Our state-of-the-art equipment is sourced from trusted partners and optimized for both small-scale and large-scale production needs, maintaining consistency and precision across all projects.
